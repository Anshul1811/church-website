History

The Grace new covenant Pentecostal church is affiliated with the Pentecostal mission, which was founded in Srilanka by Pastor. Paul Kutty in 1923. It now has churches in over 65 countries operating under various names. The church exists to bring all people everywhere to the saving knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ through the proclamation of the gospel and equipping believers for every God- glorifying service

Our Mission

To glorify God by promoting in the lives of all people, thoughts, words, and actions that conform to the standard taught and exemplified by Jesus Christ

To proclaim the whole truth of God’s Word as shown in Holy Scripture so that people will be born again into the Kingdom of God and so that born again believers will lead victorious, Holy Spirit directed lives as members of the Kingdom of God

To teach all people to worship God in spirit and in truth.

About the Church Pastor / Other Servants of God

The Church is led by Pastor. Shaji Mangalath and Sister. Annie Mangalath. They felt a strong call to strive for God’s kingdom, left everything to follow Jesus in order to pursue righteousness, their Faith in God and relentless prayer were the two most important factors in building the Church. They minister along with the other two full time ministers Sis. Karen Maclean and Sis. Erika and they discipline the church to prepare the believers for the kingdom of God.
